These concepts are the foundation of maintainable AI-assisted coding:

1. **Skills Paradigm** - Architectural evolution beyond agents
2. **Verification** - Ensures AI isn't faking actions (prevents disasters)

**Without these fundamentals, you risk:**
- Building "mystery code" you can't maintain
- AI creating fake console.log statements
- 3-4 hours to add simple features (vs. 5 minutes)
